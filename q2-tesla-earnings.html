Tesla Earnings

published Date: Jul 2024 |  < 1 minute read



Here are four key points from Tesla's earnings report:

  • Tesla reported Q2 revenue of $25.05 billion, slightly above the expected $24.63 billion and the previous year's $24.93 billion.
  • Adjusted EPS for Q2 was $0.52, below the expected $0.60, with $1.8 billion in non-GAAP net income.
  • Tesla delivered 443,956 vehicles globally in Q2, surpassing the 439,302 estimate but down nearly 5% from the previous year. However, this was a significant increase from Q1's 386,810 deliveries.
  • Tesla deployed 9.4 GWh of battery energy storage in Q2, its highest quarterly amount ever and more than double the deployment in Q1.
