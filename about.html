about

Welcome to AiMeRaKi, where topics of finance, technology, and the economy converge to maximize wealth. Our mission is to empower you to rethink traditional income sources and embrace investment as the primary driver of financial growth. Here, We believe in transforming perspectives by shifting from a paycheck-centric view to an investment-centric mindset, unlocking the potential for every expense to contribute to your growing wealth.

At AiMeRaKi, we provide the knowledge and tools necessary to master this transformative approach. By blending insights from finance, technology, and the economy, we guide you in harnessing the power of smart investments, innovative financial technologies, and strategic economic understanding. Our aim is to inspire and equip you to build a resilient and prosperous financial future, proving that while the math may be the same, the mindset makes all the difference.

The name AiMeRaKi symbolizes our core values: “aimer,” the French word for love, and “meraki,” a Greek term for infusing passion, soul, and creativity into what you do. This fusion represents our commitment to excellence and innovation in AI, tech, and finance, and our dedication to loving what we do wholeheartedly.

My personal journey began with a deep fascination for technology, leading me to create an ID card application for the Olympics. This experience highlighted the value of data and user experience, propelling me into the realm of finance, data management and business intelligence. As technology evolved, I delved into data visualization and machine learning, striving to develop accurate and scalable models. My role as a data management lead at OMB/Whitehouse was a pivotal moment, where I oversaw federal budget, multiple data platforms and influenced federal policies through impactful white papers and memos.

Throughout my career, I evolved as a leader, learning the importance of patience, collaboration, and trust. Managing and leading teams required a new skill set, emphasizing the need for patient listening and team support. This leadership evolution taught me that respect and trust are earned through more than just technical expertise.

Through AiMeRaKi, I share my expertise and insights, inviting you to join me on this enthralling journey into the world of technology and finance. Together, we can redefine wealth creation, embracing a future where financial growth is driven by strategic investments.

Welcome to AiMeRaKi, where passion meets innovation in the pursuit of maximizing wealth.
